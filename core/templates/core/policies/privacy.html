Privacy Policy

Your Data, Your Privacy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, list a crop, apply for a scheme, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, location, and details about your farm and crops.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including providing tailored crop advice, facilitating marketplace transactions, and processing scheme applications. We may also use your data to analyze trends and improve the AgroSense AI models.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing (Razorpay) and data analysis. We may also share information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of AgroSense and its users.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging into your account settings. You can also request to delete your account by contacting our support team.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, providing you with additional notice.
