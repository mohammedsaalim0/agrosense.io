Shipping Policy

Reliable Delivery to Your Farm

1. Shipping Areas

We ship agriculture inputs, seeds, and tools across all major states in India. For remote village locations, we partner with specialized rural logistics providers to ensure your order reaches your doorstep.

2. Processing Time

All orders are processed within 24-48 hours. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or public holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days.

3. Delivery Estimates

Standard delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. We offer free shipping on orders above ₹1000.

5. Damaged Shipments

AgroSense is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. However, if you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier or our support team directly with photos of the damaged packaging and product to file a claim.
